# Staff Quickstart Guide

**Audience:** Appraisee (staff member being appraised)  
**System:** Performance Management System (PMS)

This guide walks you through every step you will take during the annual performance cycle.

---

## Step 1 — Accepting Your Invite and Logging In

1. Check your work email for a message with the subject "You've been invited to...".
2. Click the link in the email. It takes you to the app's account-setup page.
3. Choose a password (minimum 10 characters, at least one uppercase and one number).
4. You are now logged in.

If your invite link has expired (links are valid for 7 days), contact HR or IT at it@invenioptl.com and ask for a new invite.

**Logging in after setup:** Visit the app URL, enter your work email and password, and click Sign In.

**Forgotten password:** Click "Forgot password?" on the login page, enter your email, and follow the reset link sent to your inbox.

---

## Step 2 — Drafting Your KRAs

Your HR team will open the KRA window when the cycle begins. You will receive an email notification.

1. Log in. Your dashboard shows a prompt: "Your FY 2027 KRA window is open."
2. Click **Set Up My KRAs**.
3. The KRA form has four perspectives: Financial, Customer, Internal Process, and Learning & Growth.
4. For each KRA you want to add, click **+ Add KRA** under the relevant perspective.
5. Fill in:
   - **Description:** What you aim to achieve (be specific).
   - **Measurement:** How progress will be measured (quantitative if possible).
   - **Target:** The specific result you aim to reach by year-end.
   - **Weight (%):** How much this KRA contributes to your overall score.
   - **Rubric:** Optionally describe what a 1-star, 3-star, and 5-star result looks like.
6. The total weight across all KRAs must sum to exactly 100%. The form shows a running total.
7. Click **Save Draft** at any time. Your draft is saved automatically every few minutes.
8. You can add, edit, or delete KRAs until you submit.

---

## Step 3 — Submitting Your KRAs for Approval

When you are satisfied with your KRAs:

1. From the KRA form, click **Submit for Approval**.
2. A confirmation dialog shows your KRA list and total weight (must be 100%).
3. Click **Confirm Submit**.
4. Your KRAs move to "Pending Approval" status.
5. Your appraiser (direct manager) receives an email notification.

You cannot edit KRAs after submission unless your appraiser rejects them and sends them back.

---

## Step 4 — Waiting for KRA Approval

Your appraiser reviews your KRAs and either:

- **Approves** them: You will receive an email. Your KRA window closes and the cycle moves forward.
- **Rejects** them with a note: You will receive an email explaining what needs to change. Log back in, review the note, make your edits, and resubmit.

---

## Step 5 — Mid-Year Self-Check

Around mid-year, HR opens the mid-year checkpoint window. You will receive an email.

1. Log in. Your dashboard shows: "Mid-year check-in is open."
2. Click **Mid-Year Check-In**.
3. For each KRA, enter:
   - **Result achieved so far:** A brief description of progress.
   - **Rating (1–5):** Your self-assessment of how you are tracking.
4. Optionally add a summary in the text box.
5. Click **Submit**.
6. Your appraiser reviews and acknowledges your mid-year update.

Your trajectory bar on the dashboard updates after mid-year ratings are entered.

---

## Step 6 — Self-Review During PMS

Towards year-end, HR opens the PMS window. You will receive an email.

1. Log in. Click **Start Self-Review** from the dashboard.
2. The PMS form has several sections:

   **Part I — KRA Ratings (self):** For each KRA, describe the final result you achieved and give yourself a rating (1–5).

   **Part II — Behavioural Dimensions (22 items):** Rate yourself on 22 competency anchors. Read the anchor description for each rating level before selecting.

   **Part III — Staff Contributions:** List significant contributions during the year (project outcomes, special assignments, etc.) with approximate dates and a relative weighting.

   **Part IV — Career Development:** Indicate your readiness for the next level (now, 1–2 years, after 2 years, etc.).

   **Part V — Personal Growth:** Describe training needs or professional development goals for next year.

   **Part VI — Your Comment:** Write a general comment about your year. This will be signed digitally.

3. Click **Save** at any time. Come back to complete sections across multiple sessions.
4. When all sections are complete, click **Submit Self-Review**.
5. Your appraiser is notified to begin their rating.

---

## Step 7 — Reviewing Your PDF

After your appraiser and (if applicable) your next-level manager complete their ratings, HR finalizes your cycle. You will receive a notification.

1. Log in. Your dashboard shows: "Your FY 2027 assessment has been finalized."
2. Click **View PDF**.
3. A download link appears (valid for 24 hours). Click it to download your final PDF.
4. The PDF contains your KRAs, all ratings, comments, and a final score.

If you need the PDF again after the link expires, log back in and click **View PDF** to generate a new link.

---

## Step 8 — Using the AI Summary Panel

If your organization has enabled AI features, you will see an **AI Summary** panel on your dashboard.

1. Click **Generate Summary** next to your current cycle.
2. The AI reads your KRAs, mid-year updates, and any available ratings to produce a brief narrative summary of your performance arc.
3. This summary is for your reference only. It is not part of the official record.
4. You can regenerate the summary if you make significant changes to your KRA ratings.
