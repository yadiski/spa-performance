# Frequently Asked Questions

**System:** Performance Management System (PMS)  
**Last updated:** 2026-04-20

---

## Login and Access

**Q1: I can't log in. What should I do?**

First, check that you are using your work email address (not a personal email). If you get "Invalid email or password":
1. Try "Forgot password?" on the login page to reset your password.
2. If the password reset email does not arrive within 5 minutes, check your spam folder.
3. If you still cannot log in, contact IT at it@invenioptl.com and include your employee number.

If you see "Your account has been locked", contact IT or HR immediately. Accounts lock after too many failed login attempts and must be unlocked by IT Admin.

---

**Q2: I forgot my password. How do I reset it?**

1. Click "Forgot password?" on the login page.
2. Enter your work email address and click Submit.
3. Check your inbox (and spam folder) for a password reset email.
4. Click the link in the email — it expires after 24 hours.
5. Enter and confirm your new password.

If you do not receive the reset email, contact IT at it@invenioptl.com.

---

**Q3: My account is locked. How do I unlock it?**

Accounts lock automatically after 10 failed login attempts. Contact IT Admin (it@invenioptl.com) or HR Admin (hr-admin@invenioptl.com) to unlock it. Please do not attempt more logins while locked — it resets the timer.

---

**Q4: I can't see my team on the manager dashboard.**

This means your direct-report links are not configured in the system. Contact IT Admin and provide your employee number and the employee numbers of your direct reports. They will update the manager hierarchy.

---

**Q5: I was added to the system but I don't have a cycle. Why?**

HR must open the KRA window for your cycle to appear. If you see "No active cycle", contact your HR Admin. If you recently joined and your record was just created, HR needs to open a cycle for you.

---

## KRAs

**Q6: Can I edit my KRAs after I submit them?**

No — once submitted, KRAs are locked. However, if your appraiser rejects them and sends them back with a note, you can edit and resubmit. After approval, KRAs cannot be changed for that cycle year.

If an approved KRA contains a critical error, contact HR Admin who can trigger an amendment workflow.

---

**Q7: My KRA weights don't sum to 100%. The form won't let me submit.**

The form requires exactly 100% total weight across all KRAs. Review each KRA's weight percentage and adjust until the total shown at the bottom reaches 100%. Common causes: forgetting to update a weight after adding or removing a KRA.

---

**Q8: I submitted my KRAs but my appraiser says they didn't receive a notification.**

Ask your appraiser to log in and check their dashboard — the pending approval appears there even without an email. If they genuinely cannot see it, contact IT Admin to check the notification job status.

---

## Mid-Year and PMS

**Q9: Why is my trajectory bar empty?**

The trajectory bar appears on your dashboard once you have at least one mid-year rating or final KRA rating. If you have not completed the mid-year checkpoint yet (or it has not been opened by HR), the bar will be empty. Once the mid-year window opens and you submit your self-check, the bar will populate.

---

**Q10: Who sees my self-rating?**

Your self-rating (for both KRAs and behavioural dimensions) is visible to:
- You
- Your appraiser (direct manager)
- Your next-level appraiser (if applicable)
- HR Admins

Your self-rating is not visible to other staff members or peers.

---

**Q11: Can I change my ratings after I click "Submit Self-Review"?**

No — once submitted, your self-review is locked and your appraiser is notified. If there is a genuine error, contact HR Admin. They can return the cycle to you if the appraiser has not yet started their rating.

---

**Q12: My appraiser submitted their rating but I disagree. What can I do?**

1. Your appraiser can choose to return the assessment to you to add a rebuttal comment before final submission to next-level. Ask your appraiser to use the "Return to Appraisee" option.
2. If the cycle has already been submitted to next-level or finalized, raise your concern with HR Admin who will advise on the amendment process.

---

**Q13: The PMS form shows 22 behavioural dimensions. Do I have to rate all of them?**

Yes — all 22 are required before you can submit your self-review. If a dimension does not apply to your role, discuss with your appraiser; they may instruct you to select the middle rating (3) with a note explaining the non-applicability.

---

## PDF and Documents

**Q14: How do I get a copy of my finalized performance review?**

After HR finalizes your cycle, you will receive an email notification. Log in and click "View PDF" on your dashboard. A download link valid for 24 hours will appear. Click it to download the PDF.

If the link has expired, log back in and click "View PDF" again to generate a new link.

---

**Q15: The PDF download link is not working. What should I do?**

Links expire after 24 hours. Log back in and click "View PDF" to get a new link. If the error says "PDF not ready", the generation job may still be processing — wait 5–10 minutes and try again. If the issue persists, contact IT at it@invenioptl.com.

---

## AI Features

**Q16: What does the AI Summary panel do?**

The AI Summary panel generates a brief narrative overview of your performance arc based on your KRAs, mid-year updates, and ratings. It is a reading aid — it does not affect your actual score or the official record. Think of it as an auto-draft of a narrative summary.

---

**Q17: The AI summary looks wrong. Does it affect my score?**

No. The AI summary is purely informational and does not change any stored data or your final score. Your score is computed from the KRA ratings, behavioural dimension ratings, and contribution weights as configured. If you are concerned about the score itself, speak with your appraiser or HR Admin.

---

## General

**Q18: My name or email in the system is wrong. How do I fix it?**

Contact IT Admin (it@invenioptl.com) with your correct name and email. They will update your records. Note: your login email must also be changed if you use it to log in.

---

**Q19: I can't see a previous year's performance review.**

Cycles from previous years are accessible if they were recorded in this system. If you started using the system this year, older cycles may not be available. Contact HR Admin to check if historical data was imported.

---

**Q20: How do I report a bug or system problem?**

Contact IT Admin at it@invenioptl.com. Include:
- Your employee number
- What you were trying to do
- What error message (or unexpected behaviour) you saw
- The approximate time it happened

For urgent issues during go-live or active cycle windows, also message HR Admin directly.
