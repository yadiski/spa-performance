# Appraiser Quickstart Guide

**Audience:** Appraiser (direct manager / supervisor)  
**System:** Performance Management System (PMS)

This guide covers every action an appraiser takes during the annual performance cycle. You will receive email notifications at each step that requires your attention.

---

## Your Role

As appraiser, you are responsible for:
1. Reviewing and approving (or rejecting) your direct reports' KRAs.
2. Acknowledging mid-year self-assessments.
3. Rating KRAs and 22 behavioural dimensions at year-end.
4. Writing and digitally signing your appraiser comment.
5. Returning the assessment to the appraisee if corrections are needed, or submitting it to next-level.

---

## Step 1 — Approving KRAs

You will receive an email when a direct report submits their KRAs.

1. Log in. Your manager dashboard shows the notification: "[Staff name] has submitted KRAs for review."
2. Click the staff member's name to open their KRA list.
3. Review each KRA:
   - Is the description specific and measurable?
   - Does the weighting seem reasonable?
   - Do the targets align with department objectives?
4. If satisfied: click **Approve KRAs**. The staff member receives an email confirmation.
5. If changes are needed: click **Reject with Note**. Enter your feedback (required). The staff member is notified and can revise and resubmit.

You can approve KRAs for multiple staff members in sequence without returning to the dashboard.

---

## Step 2 — Acknowledging Mid-Year Updates

HR will open the mid-year window. Your direct reports complete self-assessments. You receive an email for each.

1. Log in. Your dashboard shows pending mid-year acknowledgements.
2. Click a staff member's name to view their mid-year update.
3. Review their progress ratings and summary.
4. If you have feedback, note it (the app may allow inline comments depending on your configuration; if not, have a 1:1 discussion).
5. Click **Acknowledge** to confirm you have reviewed the update.
6. The cycle moves forward to `mid_year_done` once you acknowledge.

You do not rate the staff member at this point — that happens during the PMS phase.

---

## Step 3 — Rating KRAs at Year-End

HR opens the PMS window. Your direct report completes their self-review first and submits it. You then receive an email.

1. Log in. Your dashboard shows: "[Staff name] has completed their self-review."
2. Click the staff member to open the PMS assessment.
3. In **Part I — KRA Ratings**, for each KRA:
   - Read the staff member's self-assessment and their stated result achieved.
   - Enter **Result Achieved** from your perspective (what you observed).
   - Select a **Final Rating (1–5)** using the rubric anchors provided.
   - Optionally add a comment.

Take your time — this is the core of the assessment. Ratings are locked once you submit.

---

## Step 4 — Rating 22 Behavioural Dimensions

After KRA ratings, the form continues to **Part II — Behavioural Dimensions**.

1. For each of the 22 dimensions, read the dimension title and description.
2. Read the anchor text for each rating level (1 = Rarely, 5 = Consistently exceeds).
3. Select the rating that best describes the staff member's behaviour during the year.
4. Optionally add a comment for context.

All 22 dimensions must be rated before you can proceed.

---

## Step 5 — Writing and Signing Your Comment

After behavioural ratings, the form reaches **Part VI — Appraiser Comment**.

1. Write a general comment about the staff member's overall performance during the year.
2. Include notable achievements, areas for growth, and any context not captured in the ratings.
3. Click **Sign Comment**. The system presents the comment text and asks you to confirm.
4. Click **Confirm Signature**. A digital signature is attached to your comment (timestamped, linked to your user account, and cryptographically chained to the audit log).

You cannot edit the comment after signing. If you need to make a change, you must contact HRA to reopen the cycle.

---

## Step 6 — Return or Submit

After signing, you have two options:

### Option A: Return to Appraisee

If you want the staff member to respond to your ratings or add a rebuttal comment before the cycle proceeds:

1. Click **Return to Appraisee**.
2. The staff member can view your ratings and add their final comment.
3. They will submit back to you, at which point you can proceed to Step 7.

### Option B: Submit to Next Level

If no further dialogue is needed:

1. Click **Submit to Next Level**.
2. The cycle moves to your line manager (next-level appraiser) or to HR finalization, depending on your organisation's configuration.
3. Your next-level manager receives an email.

---

## Step 7 — Handling a Return from Next Level

If your next-level manager returns the assessment for revision:

1. You receive an email: "[Next-level manager] has returned [staff name]'s assessment."
2. Log in and open the assessment.
3. Review the next-level manager's note.
4. You can update your ratings and comment (comment will need to be re-signed).
5. Resubmit to next level when ready.

---

## Manager Dashboard

Your manager dashboard (accessible from the top navigation) shows:
- All direct reports and the current state of their cycles
- Pending actions: KRA approvals, mid-year acknowledgements, PMS ratings due
- Cycle state at a glance: not started, KRA submitted, mid-year open, PMS in progress, finalized

Click any staff member's row to open their current form or cycle detail.

---

## Quick Reference

| Action | When | Where |
|--------|------|-------|
| Approve/reject KRAs | After staff submits | Dashboard → staff row → Approve KRAs |
| Acknowledge mid-year | After staff submits mid-year | Dashboard → staff row → Acknowledge |
| Rate KRAs | After staff submits self-review | Dashboard → staff row → Rate KRAs |
| Rate behavioural dimensions | During KRA rating session | PMS form → Part II |
| Write + sign comment | After rating all dimensions | PMS form → Part VI |
| Return to appraisee | Optional, before submitting | PMS form → Return to Appraisee |
| Submit to next level | After signing comment | PMS form → Submit to Next Level |
| Download PDF (after finalization) | After HRA finalizes | Dashboard → staff row → View PDF |
